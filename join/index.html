Awesome information:

The Volunteers will be picked up from the Airport and will be taken care of from the day they arrive till there departure.
All meals will be prepared for the volunteers and many extras.

We are doing 4 trips with the volunteers to make an awesome experience in such a beautiful country. (optional).

  1. Safari 2 days
  2. Whale watching
  3. Elephant feeding
  4. Beach house to relax and enjoy the beach after so much work done
  5. Swimming In pool or at the waterfall
And many more....

Accommodation and food:

Volunteers are accommodated on the farm with a beautiful garden and swimming pool. There will be 2 volunteers per room and each room with a bathroom. The rooms are very comfortable and all volunteers need to do is arrive with there personal belongings. The project offers a full laundry service. The volunteers will have all meals prepared for them and eat with the family living at the project.

Final Step:

  • Fill in the application form and mail it back to us.
  • We will send you per mail to confirm your place in the group.
  • After you have received the confirmation you have to pay the deposit and establish your flight.
  • Payment should be 60 days prior to your arrival date.
  • Once you have booked with us, your dates have been confirmed and you have paid your deposit we will send the necessary information to help you prepare your trip and stay with us.
