Program 1

A Day in the life of our Volunteers

Early Bird ( Surprise) 2x per week
07:30 – 08:00
Coffee and Tee

09:00 - 09:30
Breakfast
09:30 – 12:30
Work starts. The volunteers will be divide into 4 Groups and each group has got his/her task that will be given and explained at the Early Bird or the day before.
12:30 – 14:00
Lunch
14:00 – 16:00
Work will be exceeded and Preparations for the next day will be done
16:00 – 16:30
Cleaning up and putting everything in place.
Free time (swimming, walking, mountain climbing, Horse riding, playing with the Animals, relax)

Program 2

Teaching in South Africa
For Passau University students

School information

  • Name: Principia Independent School
  • Location: Oudtshoorn and Klaarstroom (Western Cape Gardenroute)
  • Type of School: Elementary
  • Age: 7-14 years
  • Class size: Between 15 and 40
  • Extra-Curricular roles: Sport, Music and Fun
  • Accommodation: Host Families or Apartments on the School premises
  • Length of placement: From 4 weeks
  • Starting date: Flexible


In South Africa there are dramatic changes and optimism. Allot of our Schools are mostly over-Crowded, understaffed and under-funded, and enthusiasm is evident and Volunteers are needed to cultivate this.


Most Teaching Volunteers get involved in at least 2 of The Main teaching areas, working as a Teacher, Assistant or participating in the literacy or music.
