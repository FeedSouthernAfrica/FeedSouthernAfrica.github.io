The Program

A Day in the life of our Volunteers
(Monday to Friday – weekends are free or you can take part in our weekend trips)

07:00 – 07:30
Early Bird ( Surprise) 2x per week
07:30 – 08:00
Coffee and Tee
08:00 – 09:00
Start preparation for days work. Getting all equipment together make sure to understand what is to be done for the day. Divide the work.
09:00 - 09:30
Breakfast
09:30 – 12:30
Work starts. The volunteers will be divide into 4 Groups and each group has got his/her task that will be given and explained at the Early Bird or the day before.
12:30 – 14:00
Lunch
14:00 – 16:00
Work will be exceeded and Preparations for the next day will be done
16:00 – 16:30
Cleaning up and putting everything in place.
Free time (swimming, walking, mountain climbing, Horse riding, playing with the Animals, relax)
