Introduction

There is absolutely no reason why any child in this world should go hungry. No excuses any more, we have the responsibility to help those children in need.

Our mission is to provide young children with one warm meal a day and provide those in need of basic education for life. This is a project where each and every one of us get the opportunity to help those in need and also become part of a family.

We are a family farm in South Africa (Western Cape) giving volunteers all over the world the opportunity to experience our country, get to know our culture and be overwhelmed by the beauty of our country.

Volunteer program info

There is something truly special about being part of a program helping those in need if you are passionate about children, nature and Africa and you want to make a difference,then this program is just for you.

Our Program will give you a fascinating outdoor and animal farm life experience. We produce vegetables and fruit from our own garden where we can teach our youngsters to provide for themselves through educating them how to garden. After sharing with them lunch we spend quality time with the kiddies playing sports, making music, drawing and many more. Our volunteers will be taken care of 24h, from the day of arrival till their departure.
