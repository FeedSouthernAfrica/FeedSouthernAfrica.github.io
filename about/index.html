Introduction

There is absolutely no reason why any child in this world should hunger. No excuses any more, we have the responsibility to help those children in need.

Our Mission is to deliver young children one warm meal a day by preparing them the food out of our own grown vegetable and fruit garden. This is a project where each and every one of us get the opportunity to help those in need and also become part of a family.

We offer anybody with the urge to make a change in a hungry child's life to join us change there life's and I guarantee you that yours will change as well

You can make the experience of a lifetime. This ist he opportunity to help us help those in need.

We are a family farm in South Africa (Western Cape) producing food to provide the children one warm meal a day and at the same time we want our Volunteers to experience a part of our country, get to know our culture and be overwhelmed by the beauty of our nature.

Volunteer program info

There is something truly special about being part of the life of a hungry child. If you are passionate about children, nature and Africa and want to make a difference in their lives then this is for you. The volunteers will help us to help those in need.

Our volunteer program will give you a fascinating outdoor and animal-farm life experience. We produce one proper meal a day out of our vegetable and fruit garden which we can bring every day to young children in a small village. After sharing with them the lunch we have prepared on the farm we spend quality time with the kiddies playing soccer or making music, drawing and many more. Our volunteers will be taking care of 24 h a day from the day they arrive till there departure.
